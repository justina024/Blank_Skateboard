About Us

Blank Skateboards is a Canada-based company founded in 2017 by Justina Chen and Yanto Liang. The boards are using high-quality plastic as the major material which is lightweight and flexible to design. The term “Blank” is synonymous the concept of customizing the skateboard starting from a clean board with creative designs. Blank Skateboards are composed of several different parts such as, deck, wheels, and trucks which are separately made.
